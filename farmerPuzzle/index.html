Rules of the Game

A farmer approaches a river and needs to cross it. He has with him a fox, a chicken and a bag of seeds. There is a boat in the river that can carry the farmer and, at most, one of the items. The farmer cannot leave the fox alone with the chicken or the fox will eat the chicken. Also, the chicken will eat the seeds if the two are together without the farmer. Use the buttons below to safely move the farmer and all his objects back and forth across the river until the farmer and the items are all safe on the opposite river bank.
