Objective of the Game

The objective of Whazzle! is to find the hidden word among the random letters and spell it out in the top row

Columns may be swapped and the letters within the columns may be rotated up and down. After the player has moved the letters to the desired locations, the button can be clicked to reduce the puzzle. Points will be added to the player's score for any reductions made to the puzzle. If the player did not correctly guess the word, the turns remaining will be reduced by one and the player will have the opportunity to guess again if they still have turns remaining. If there are still further puzzles to play after the current puzzle ends, the button will change into the button.

Color Codes

The columns can be one of three colors: white, blue or green. Some of the letters in the columns may also be bolded.

The white columns indicate that the column is not in the correct position. These columns may be swapped with other white columns and the letters within the column can be rotated. Blue columns are in the correct location but the letter at the top of the column is not correct. Blue columns are locked in place and cannot be swapped with other columns. The letters in blue columns can still be rotated. Green columns have been solved and cannot be further manipulated. Only a single letter will appear in a green column. Bolded letters indicate the letters will be found somewhere in the solution.
